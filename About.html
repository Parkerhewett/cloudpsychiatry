Our Values

Our Care Philosophy

Four core principles guide everything we do at Cloud Psychiatry

01

Evidence-Based Practice

Every treatment decision is grounded in current clinical research and proven therapeutic methods — not guesswork or trends.

02

Whole-Person Care

Mental health doesn't exist in isolation. We consider your biology, environment, relationships, and goals together as one picture.

03

Collaborative Partnership

You are an expert on your own life. We collaborate — not dictate — so that your voice shapes every step of your treatment.

04

Accessible & Inclusive

Quality psychiatric care should be available to everyone. We work hard to reduce barriers to access and welcome all backgrounds.

The Practice

About Cloud Psychiatry

Cloud Psychiatry was established with the belief that mental health care should feel as approachable as it is effective. We've created an environment — both in-person and through telehealth — that prioritizes privacy, dignity, and ease of access.

We serve adults and adolescents across a broad range of mental health concerns, from everyday stress and anxiety to complex psychiatric conditions requiring specialized medication management and therapeutic support.

  • Board-certified psychiatric care
  • In-person & telehealth appointments available
  • Most major insurance plans accepted
  • HIPAA-compliant, secure patient portal
  • Flexible scheduling including early/late slots
  • New patients typically seen within one week
