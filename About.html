Meet Your Provider

Sarah Cloud, MSN, APRN, PMHNP-BC

Board-Certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner & Founder, Cloud Psychiatric Care, LLC
"I help my patients to understand why they think, feel and act the way that they do, and then treat them with evidence-based care accordingly."

Sarah Cloud is a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP) and the founder of Cloud Psychiatric Care, LLC. She earned her Master of Science in Nursing from the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences (UAMS), where her dedication to psychiatric care was recognized by colleagues and peers alike.

Sarah is passionate about caring for the whole person with a focus on mental health. She places an emphasis on the most up-to-date evidence-based research in combination with individualized care that is specific to each patient — including what will work best for them and their goals of treatment.

Her practice specializes in psychiatric assessment, diagnostics, and medication management for adults 18 and older. She supports and treats patients with a wide range of concerns and diagnoses including depression, anxiety, PTSD, bipolar disorders, ADHD, sleep disorders, OCD, PMDD, schizoaffective disorder, eating disorders, and postpartum mood issues.

Sarah treats patients in Arkansas and Oklahoma exclusively via secure telehealth, making high-quality psychiatric care accessible no matter where her patients are located. Her goal is to work together with each patient to help them live their healthiest and happiest life.

Our Values

Sarah's Care Philosophy

Four principles that guide every patient relationship at Cloud Psychiatric Care

01

Evidence-Based Treatment

Every diagnosis and medication decision is grounded in the most current psychiatric research — not trends. Sarah stays current so your care stays optimal.

02

Understanding the Whole You

Sarah helps patients understand why they think, feel, and act the way they do. Insight is the foundation of lasting treatment — not just symptom suppression.

03

Individualized Care

Your treatment plan is specific to you — your goals, your history, your life. What works best for you is always the starting point, not a one-size-fits-all protocol.

04

Accessible by Design

By operating entirely via telehealth in Arkansas and Oklahoma, Sarah removes the barriers of distance, transportation, and scheduling that keep people from getting care.

The Practice

About Cloud Psychiatric Care, LLC

Cloud Psychiatric Care, LLC is a fully virtual psychiatric practice founded and led by Sarah Cloud, MSN, APRN, PMHNP. The practice was built on a simple but powerful belief: that high-quality psychiatric care should be accessible to everyone — without the barriers of distance, long wait times, or difficult schedules.

Sarah specializes in psychiatric assessment, diagnostics, and medication management for adults 18 and older. This is not a therapy practice — Sarah's focus is precise clinical evaluation and evidence-based medication treatment, ensuring patients get the right diagnosis and the right treatment plan from day one.

  • 100% telehealth — no in-person visits required
  • Serving adults 18+ in Arkansas and Oklahoma
  • Secure appointments via AzaleaHealth platform
  • Assessment, diagnostics & medication management
  • Court-ordered evaluations & litigation consulting
  • Most major insurance plans accepted
  • Self-pay, HSA & FSA accepted
