Assessment, Diagnostics & Medication Management

Service Details

What to Expect

Each service is described in detail below. All appointments are conducted via secure telehealth through AzaleaHealth.

🧠

Psychiatric Evaluation

Your first visit begins with a comprehensive psychiatric evaluation. Sarah takes the time to understand your full history — medical, psychiatric, family, and social — to build an accurate clinical picture. She reviews your symptoms, any prior treatments, and your personal goals to develop a thoughtful, individualized plan rooted in evidence-based care.

This evaluation is the foundation of all care that follows. You'll leave with a clear diagnosis (when applicable), a medication treatment plan, and a roadmap for next steps.

Initial Visit Initial Session: $350 Telehealth via AzaleaHealth Adults 18+ Only
💊

Medication Management

For patients who benefit from psychiatric medication, Sarah provides ongoing management with close attention to effectiveness, side effects, and overall wellbeing. She takes a conservative, collaborative approach — explaining every option, answering questions, and always prioritizing the right medication at the right dose for you.

Follow-up visits are scheduled regularly to monitor your progress and make adjustments as your needs evolve. Medication management is Sarah's primary clinical focus, and she brings the most current evidence-based protocols to every appointment.

Follow-Up Visits Standard Visit: $175 Telehealth via AzaleaHealth
🌟

Anxiety & Depression Treatment

Anxiety and depression are among the most common — and most treatable — mental health conditions. Sarah specializes in accurate diagnosis and evidence-based medication management for mood and anxiety disorders, providing close follow-up to ensure your treatment is working and making adjustments when it's not.

Conditions treated include generalized anxiety disorder (GAD), major depressive disorder (MDD), persistent depressive disorder, panic disorder, social anxiety, and related mood conditions.

Anxiety Depression Mood Disorders
⚡

ADHD Evaluation & Treatment

ADHD often goes undiagnosed well into adulthood, impacting work, relationships, and quality of life. Sarah offers comprehensive ADHD assessment including clinical interviews and behavioral history review to confirm diagnosis. She then works with patients on evidence-based medication management — including stimulant and non-stimulant options — tailored to your individual needs and goals.

Adults 18+ Diagnostic Assessment Stimulant & Non-Stimulant Options
🛡️

PTSD & Trauma

Trauma can take many forms, and healing requires a safe, knowledgeable approach. Sarah provides trauma-informed psychiatric care and evidence-based medication management for Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and trauma-related conditions. Her approach prioritizes your safety and pace throughout the treatment process.

PTSD Trauma-Informed Medication Management
🎭

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder requires careful, ongoing psychiatric oversight. Sarah provides thorough diagnostic evaluation and evidence-based medication management for Bipolar I, Bipolar II, and related mood cycling conditions. Close monitoring and regular follow-up ensure your mood stabilization regimen is safe and effective over time.

Bipolar I & II Mood Stabilization Ongoing Monitoring
🌀

OCD Treatment

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder can significantly impact daily functioning. Sarah evaluates and manages OCD using evidence-based psychiatric medication approaches, working collaboratively with patients to reduce symptoms and improve quality of life.

OCD Evidence-Based Medication Management
🌙

Sleep Disorders

Sleep and mental health are deeply interconnected — poor sleep worsens nearly every psychiatric condition. Sarah evaluates and treats sleep disorders including insomnia and sleep disturbances related to anxiety, depression, PTSD, and other conditions. Treatment may include medication management alongside sleep hygiene guidance.

Insomnia Sleep & Mood Medication Management
🌸

PMDD & Postpartum Mood

Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder (PMDD) and postpartum mood conditions — including postpartum depression and anxiety — can be debilitating without proper treatment. Sarah provides compassionate, evidence-based evaluation and medication management for these conditions in a supportive, non-judgmental environment.

PMDD Postpartum Depression Postpartum Anxiety
⚖️

Court-Ordered Evaluations & Litigation Consulting

Sarah conducts court-ordered psychiatric evaluations with the clinical rigor and thorough documentation required for legal proceedings. She also offers litigation consulting services, providing expert psychiatric perspective to legal teams navigating cases involving mental health.

All evaluations are conducted professionally, objectively, and in compliance with applicable legal and ethical standards.

Court-Ordered Evaluations Litigation Consulting Expert Consultation
💻

Telehealth — AR & OK

All appointments at Cloud Psychiatric Care are conducted via AzaleaHealth, a secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth platform. Whether you're in Little Rock, Tulsa, or anywhere in between, Sarah can provide the same high-quality psychiatric care without requiring you to travel.

You'll need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera and a stable internet connection. A secure appointment link is sent to you ahead of each visit.
