Service Details

What to Expect

Each service is described in detail below so you know exactly what to expect when you walk through our doors

🧠

Psychiatric Evaluation

Your first visit begins with a comprehensive psychiatric evaluation. During this 60–90 minute session, we take the time to understand your full history — medical, psychiatric, family, and social — to build an accurate picture of your mental health. We review symptoms, prior treatments, and your personal goals to create a thoughtful, individualized plan.

This evaluation serves as the foundation of all care that follows. You'll leave with a clear diagnosis (when applicable), a treatment plan, and a roadmap for next steps.

Initial Visit 60–90 min In-person & Telehealth
💊

Medication Management

For patients who may benefit from psychiatric medication, we provide ongoing medication management with close attention to efficacy, side effects, and overall wellbeing. We take a conservative, collaborative approach — explaining every option, answering every question, and always prioritizing the lowest effective dose.

Follow-up visits are scheduled regularly to monitor progress and make adjustments as needed. We never "set it and forget it" — your response to medication is continuously evaluated.

Follow-Up Visits 20–30 min In-person & Telehealth
💭

Talk Therapy & Psychotherapy

We offer evidence-based psychotherapy approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT), and supportive counseling. Therapy is available as a standalone service or in combination with medication management.

Our therapeutic relationships are built on trust, confidentiality, and genuine partnership. Whether you're working through a life transition, managing a chronic condition, or just feeling stuck, therapy provides a structured space to grow and heal.

Individual Therapy 45–60 min CBT · DBT · MBCT
🌟

Anxiety & Depression Treatment

Anxiety and depression are among the most common mental health conditions, yet they are highly treatable. We specialize in integrated care for mood disorders, combining medication management (when appropriate) with evidence-based therapeutic approaches. Our goal is not just symptom relief — it's sustained recovery and a better quality of life.

We treat generalized anxiety disorder (GAD), panic disorder, social anxiety, major depressive disorder (MDD), persistent depressive disorder (PDD), and related conditions.

Mood Disorders Anxiety Depression
⚡

ADHD Evaluation & Treatment

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) affects both children and adults, often going undiagnosed for years. We offer comprehensive ADHD assessments that include clinical interviews, behavioral rating scales, and collateral history to confirm the diagnosis. Treatment options include medication, behavioral strategies, and skill-building for organization, focus, and emotional regulation.

We also support adults who were diagnosed in childhood and are navigating ADHD in the context of demanding careers, relationships, and responsibilities.

Adults & Adolescents Diagnostic Testing Stimulant & Non-Stimulant Options
🛡️

Trauma-Focused Care & PTSD

Trauma can take many forms — and healing from it requires a safe, specialized approach. We provide trauma-informed psychiatric care and evidence-based treatments including Trauma-Focused CBT (TF-CBT), EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing), and Prolonged Exposure therapy for Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and complex trauma.

Our trauma care philosophy is built on safety, stabilization, and respect for your pace. You are always in control of your healing process.

PTSD Complex Trauma EMDR Available
🌙

Sleep Disorders

Sleep and mental health are deeply interconnected. We evaluate and treat sleep disorders including insomnia, hypersomnia, circadian rhythm disruptions, and sleep difficulties related to anxiety, depression, or PTSD. Treatment may include Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia (CBT-I), sleep hygiene coaching, and when appropriate, medication management.

Insomnia CBT-I Sleep & Mood
💻

Telehealth Psychiatry

We offer secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth appointments for most of our services. Whether you prefer the convenience of home-based care, live too far from our office, or have mobility challenges, telehealth brings the same quality psychiatric care directly to you via video visit. Most insurance plans cover telehealth services at the same rate as in-person visits.

Video Visit HIPAA-Compliant Most Insurance Accepted
