About the Practice

Welcome to Cloud Psychiatric Care

Cloud Psychiatric Care, LLC is a fully virtual psychiatric practice serving adults 18 and older in Arkansas and Oklahoma. Led by Sarah Cloud, MSN, APRN, PMHNP, the practice specializes in assessment, diagnostics, and medication management — not therapy.

Sarah is passionate about caring for the whole person with a focus on mental health. She places an emphasis on the most up-to-date evidence-based research in combination with individualized care that is specific for you, including what will work best for you and your goals of treatment.

📋 What to Prepare for Your First Telehealth Visit

  • A device with a camera and stable internet connection (smartphone, tablet, or computer)
  • Insurance card — Sarah will ask for this upon scheduling
  • A list of all current medications and dosages
  • Relevant past medical and psychiatric history
  • Any questions or concerns you'd like to discuss
